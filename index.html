About Me

I’m Nittyshry Prabhu, born on January 28, 2005, in Bhagalpur, Bihar, India. Right now, I’m pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (AI & ML) from a college in Delhi, affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. I like to think of myself as a blend of logic and creativity — a passionate writer, a creative imagineer, and an aspiring engineer who balances analytical thinking with a love for words.

My Creative Side & Writings Writing has always been more than just a hobby for me — it’s my way of understanding the world and myself. Over time, I’ve penned blog posts, essays, and literary pieces that carry fragments of my own life and thoughts.

One of my most personal works is “Behind the Beautiful Lady”, an autobiographical piece where I share my mother’s unwavering support and my own dreams, struggles, and growth. My essays — “Undiscovered Me”, “Mitti Ki Khushboo”, and “The Process of Our Birth” — dive into themes of self-discovery, family bonds, and philosophical reflection. They’re as much about telling a story as they are about feeling it.

My Journey & Aspirations I studied in my home village, Bhramarpur, until high school, and then moved to Delhi for my undergraduate studies. Technology, especially AI and Machine Learning, fascinates me — but my heart also finds a home in literature. I owe a lot of my resilience and ambition to my parents, who has been my constant source of encouragement.

So far, I’ve written two books,


1. A Diary [ A Lot Words ]
2. Slip of Mind's Pen
