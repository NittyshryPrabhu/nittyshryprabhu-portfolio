Educational Journey

Early Childhood Education

Early learning stage where I attend Anganwadi or nursery schools to develop basic speaking, counting, and social skills through fun activities.

Primary Education

I learn reading, writing, basic math, and general awareness. This builds the foundation for future learning in government or private schools.

Secondary Education

I prepare for the Matriculation Exam under the Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB). Focus is on strengthening core subjects for future career choices.

Higher Secondary / Intermediate Education

I choose a stream — Science based on my interest. This stage decides the direction of higher studies or professional goals.
